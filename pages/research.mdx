# Research

Discover the latest insights in the Web3 landscape. At BLOCKDEF, we strive to provide the most up-to-date and comprehensive research materials focused on EVM smart contract vulnerabilities. Our resources include expert insights, practical guides, and in-depth analyses to help you navigate the complexities of blockchain security and smart contract development.

## Articles

Explore our extensive collection of articles written by industry experts and community members. Our articles cover a wide range of topics, from the basics of blockchain technology to advanced security practices specifically for EVM environments. Whether you are a novice developer or an experienced security researcher, you will find valuable information to enhance your knowledge and skills.

- **EVM Fundamentals**: Understand the core concepts of the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) and its role in executing smart contracts.
- **Smart Contract Security**: Learn about common vulnerabilities in EVM smart contracts, such as reentrancy attacks, integer overflows, and underflows, and best practices for mitigating them.
- **Emerging Threats**: Stay updated on the latest security threats and trends in the EVM ecosystem, including new types of attacks and vulnerabilities.
- **Regulatory Insights**: Gain insights into the regulatory landscape affecting EVM-based blockchain projects and how it impacts security practices.

## DeFi and Governance Research

Explore research focused on decentralized finance (DeFi) and governance within EVM-based ecosystems. Understand the unique challenges and security considerations in these rapidly evolving sectors.

- **DeFi Security**: Investigate common vulnerabilities and security issues in DeFi protocols, including liquidity pool exploits, flash loan attacks, and oracle manipulation.
- **Governance Models**: Study various governance models used in decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) and other blockchain-based governance structures, focusing on their strengths, weaknesses, and security implications.
- **Economic Attacks**: Learn about economic attacks in DeFi, such as front-running, sandwich attacks, and other forms of market manipulation, along with strategies to mitigate these risks.
- **Protocol Audits**: Access detailed audits of popular DeFi protocols and governance mechanisms, highlighting potential vulnerabilities and areas for improvement.
